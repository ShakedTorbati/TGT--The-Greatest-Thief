Welcome to the Online Clue Game! Objective: Uncover the identity of the thief among four suspects. Gameplay: Navigate through the rooms in order: Starting from the top left corner, proceed to the bottom right corner, visiting three rooms in total. Gather clues: In each room, you will find a clue that you must memorize. Solve the mystery: Once you have all three clues, proceed to the final interrogation room to identify the thief.
