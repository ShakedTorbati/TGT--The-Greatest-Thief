Welcome to the Online Clue Game! Objective: Uncover the identity of the thief among four suspects. Gameplay: Navigate through the rooms in order: Starting from the top left corner, proceed to the bottom right corner, visiting three rooms in total. Gather clues: In each room, you will find a clue that you must memorize. Solve the mystery: Once you have all three clues, proceed to the final interrogation room to identify the thief. Story: In the heart of the bustling city, a daring heist has taken place! A priceless artifact has vanished from the renowned Oakhaven Manor, and suspicion falls upon four notorious individuals: Professor Plum: The eccentric genius, known for his love of puzzles and riddles. Ms. Scarlet: The glamorous socialite, always dressed to the nines and hiding a secret past. Colonel Mustard: The gruff military veteran, with a reputation for discipline and a keen eye for detail. Reverend Green: The pious clergyman, with a hidden passion for adventure and a knack for deception. As a team of intrepid detectives, you have been summoned to unravel the mystery. Your mission is to navigate through the many rooms of the manor, decipher the cryptic clues left behind, and expose the cunning culprit. Embark on your quest, detectives! The fate of Oakhaven Manor's legacy rests in your hands.
